Boss Recommendations

Elden Ring has a total of 157 bosses for players to encounter and challenge and 15 of them are main bosses but from those 157 bosses, these 5 are the best main bosses that players need to fight.

1)Godfrey, First Elden Lord

Godfrey, the First ELden Lord is one of the final bosses in the game that you must fight to progress in the story. The boss fight can be split into 2 phases. Godfrey has many attacks that deal a lot of damage but are slow so if you can get your timing down, you will be able to dodge them but if you get hit, they'll deal a lot of damage. During the fight, you are allowed to use spirit ashes which if you used properly can make this fight way easier as his A.I. will focus either on you or the spirit ashes when he attacks but not only are spirit ashes allowed, you are also allowed to use multiplayer in the fight meaning you can lower the difficulty of the fight but fighting him with another person. Godfrey has 2 faces. In the first phase, he fights as Godfrey, First Elden Lord but when you get him down to 50% hp, he becames Hoarah Loux. The reason he's first on the list is that the fight with Godfrey is amazing from his lore to his many combo moves to the music of the fight and even the arena, the fight is difficult but a great one nonetheless and is worthy of being an endgame boss for players to fight. Upon defeating him, the player can use his rememberance to craft the Ash of War: Hoarah Loux's Earthshaker or Axe of Godfrey.

When you get Godrick to half health, he will cut off his arm and replace it with a dragon head. Not only is he amazing, the whole arena is amazing as well. The arnea is like a graveyard with many tomobstone but on one side, there is the literal corpse of a dragon just there which Godrick replaces one of his hands with its head. The fight helps playrs to learn how to use the environemnt to their advantage as you can user the tombstones and the trees in the arena to help block and dodge his long range attacks. IF you want to make the fight easier, you can also use multiplayer and spirit ashes in the fight. Players can use his remembrance to craft the axe of Godrick or the grafted dragon.
