About Elden Ring:

Elden Ring is a dark fantasy action RPG(Role-Playing Game) produced by FromSoftware and Hidetaka Miyazaki who also produced other famous games like Demon's Souls, the Dark Souls series, Bloodborne, and Sekiro: Shadows Die Twice. The game was revealed in E3 2019 and author George R.R. Martin, author of Game of Thrones, was involved in the game. The gamr is a soulslike game which are action role-playing games which are known for high difficulty, emphasis on environmental storytelling, deep worldbuilding, and games where the player improves by using skill and strategy but also dying a lot in the process. The story of the game is that your character, a Tarnished, who have lost the grace of the Erdtree and exiled from the Lands Between, and is summoned back after the Elden Ring shattered and the player must traverse the Lands Between to find the Great Runes, restore the Elden Ring, and become the Elden Lord.
